About Us

A small business with a big fleet — and bigger commitment.

V & A Carriers cc has been serving customers across Cape Town and the Western Cape for over two decades. Our fleet of MAN trucks, crane trucks and curtain-side trailers means we're equipped to handle a wide range of loads — from pallets of bricks to bulk freight.

What sets us apart is simple: experienced drivers, well-maintained vehicles, and the personal service you only get from a family-run business.
